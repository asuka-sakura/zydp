zapping your classic dream pets ♡


hi hello, welcome to zapping your classic dream pets! this is an NPC version of zapping your dream pets from retail neo. (disclaimer: i am not associated w/ them besides being a member, just wanted to create something similar for our NPC community! ♡)

guidelines ♡


  • only submit wishes from one account.
  • you may list up to 1 wish at a time.
  • only wish for and adopt pets that you intend to KEEP.
  • please respond if a zapper contacts you about your wish, even if you do not decide to adopt the pet.
  • be polite, this is meant to be for fun/supporting the community.
  • we reserve the right to NOT list your username.
  • wish priority is first come first serve.
  • we are not responsible for misses in the pound. (will be moot once transfers are added)
  • please do not be name picky - name change tokens (thankfully) exist on NPC as opposed to retail.
  • adding your dream pet to this list does NOT guarantee that you will get a pet.
